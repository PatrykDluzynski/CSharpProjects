Who am I?

My name is Patryk Dluzynski, and I am 22 years old. I was born in Poland, but have been living in Ireland since I was 9!

My hobbies and interests

My main hobby is pc gaming. As my parents have told me I've been gaming almost since I was born. My favourite game of all time is Hollow Knight.

While gaming is partially the reason why I started to learn how to program, my ultimate goal is to learn how to create machine learning algorithms and hopefully build a career around them.

I also love listening to music, especially electronic, and "chill" genres, and my favourite artist is Gramatik.

Physics and chemistry interest me as well, and I finished a 4 year course in Nanoscience in Trinity College, Dublin, but ultimately found that I'm more interested in computer science.
